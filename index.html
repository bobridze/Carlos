Refining Beauty, Restoring Confidence

Dr. Medina completed college at the University of Puerto Rico, where he developed an interest in medicine. He published 17 manuscripts, presented at 16 national meetings, and co-authored a surgery book chapter. Now, he runs a boutique cosmetic surgery practice specializing in mommy makeovers, liposuction, facelifts, and reconstructive procedures.
